Research

My focal interest is in ecological crime dynamics. Currently, my work focuses on drug markets and violent crime dynamics, particularly at the neighborhood level. I'm also interested in policing and sources of informal social control in communities. Methodologically, I am interested in the application of quantitative and computational techniques to questions in criminology.
